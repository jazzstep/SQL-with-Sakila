Hi I'm Jazz!

Jazz is research study coordinator with the FAB 400 study. Jazz graduated from Northwestern University with a major in psychology and a minor in gender studies. While at Northwestern, she completed an honors thesis examining sexual minority identity development. She has also worked on research projects examining prejudice and stereotyping, as well as body image and self-esteem. Jazz has been involved in sexual assault activism, as well as organizing community spaces for LBT folks. She hopes to one day attend graduate school in social psychology.
